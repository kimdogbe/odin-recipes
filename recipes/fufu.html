Fufu

Description

Ghana style fufu recipe with light soup and chicken.

Ingredients

  • Casava
  • Plantain
  • Chicken
  • Chicken Stock
  • Onions
  • Bell Pepper
  • Scotch Bonnet Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil plantain and Casava until cooked
  2. Add to mortar and pound until starching and stretchy
  3. Fry onions and peppers
  4. Add stock and chicken
  5. Bring to a boil
  6. Add fufu ball to soup and enjoy!
